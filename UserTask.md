# User Tutorials
Step-by-step tutorials on how to use the MyFavoriteAlbums site.

## Navigating MyFavoriteAlbums  
The landing page is the **Home** page.   
To make your way around the site, look under the *MyFavoriteAlbums* header to find each tab. By clicking on any of the tabs you are brought to their respective pages. The same UI remains in place for this navigation.

## Using MyFavoriteAlbums  
### Number One Albums  
A slider with 2 circles can be seen along a bar with years labeled at intervals. Move the circles to the desired labeled year range.  
![tasknumberonealbums1.png](/images/tasknumberonealbums1.png) 
Example:  
Find the \#1 albums of 2006-2009

1. Move the right circle to the line labeled 2009  
   ![tasknumberonealbums2.png](/images/tasknumberonealbums2.png) 
2. Move the left circle until the blue textbox above the circle says **2006**  
   ![tasknumberonealbums3.png](/images/tasknumberonealbums3.png) 

> Tip: Always move the right circle to your end year first, then the left circle for the starting year.

### Top Albums by Year  
A box containing the earliest year an album released in the data is shown. 

1. Click on the box to open a dropdown menu.  
2. Select the year you want to see the top albums for.  
3. Press **Submit** to show the rank of albums for the year.

![tasktopalbums1.png](/images/tasktopalbums1.png)   
Example:  
Find the top albums of 2007 

1. Click on the box containing **1993**. A drop down menu appears.

![tasktopalbums2.png](/images/tasktopalbums2.png)   

2. Scroll down and click on **2007**.  
   ![tasktopalbums3.png](/images/tasktopalbums3.png)     
3. Click **Submit**. The top ten should look like the following figure:  
   ![tasktopalbums4.png](/images/tasktopalbums4.png)   

After **Submit** has been clicked, the list changes whenever you choose another year.

### Artists’ Albums  
A box containing an artist’s name is shown. 

1. Click on the box to open a dropdown menu.  
2. Select the artist you want.   
3. Press **Submit** to show albums by the artist.

Example:  
Finding Daft Punk’s albums

1. Click on the box containing **10,000 Maniacs**![][image9]  
2. Scroll down and click on **Daft Punk**.  
   ![taskartistsalbums1.png](/images/taskartistsalbums1.png)   
3. Click **Submit**. All albums in the dataset are shown alongside their release years and ratings.  
   ![taskartistsalbums2.png](/images/taskartistsalbums2.png)   

Once **Submit** has been clicked, the list changes whenever you choose another artist.

### Favorite Artists  
A box containing **10** is shown. 

1. Click on the box to open a dropdown menu containing numbers 1-10.   
2. Select a minimum number of albums  
3. Press **Submit** to show the top 15 rated artists with a minimum number of albums of the number you selected.

The checkbox filters EPs and Live Albums from the ranking if selected.

Example:  
Find top artists with a minimum of 9 albums  
![taskfavoriteartsts1.png](/images/taskfavoriteartists1.png)   

1. Click on the box containing **10**

![taskfavoriteartsts2.png](/images/taskfavoriteartists2.png)

2. Click on **9**

![taskfavoriteartsts3.png](/images/taskfavoriteartists3.png)

3. Click **Submit**. The top ranking artists with a minimum of 9 albums are displayed

![taskfavoriteartsts4.png](/images/taskfavoriteartists4.png)
### Artist Comparison  
Two boxes containing dropdown menus are shown. 

1. Click on a box and select an artist.This show their album ratings over the years on the line graph.   
2. Select another artist to display their ratings alongside the first artist’s ratings. 

![taskartistcomparison1.png](/images/taskartistcomparison1.png) 
![taskartistcomparison2.png](/images/taskartistcomparison2.png)
Example:  
Compare Taylor Swift with Bon Iver’s album ratings

1. Click on the first box to open the dropdown menu  
2. Scroll down and select **Taylor Swift**  
   ![taskartistcomparison3.png](/images/taskartistcomparison3.png)  
3. Click on the second box to open the dropdown menu  
4. Scroll down and select **Bon Iver**  
   ![taskartistcomparison4.png](/images/taskartistcomparison4.png) 
* The graph displays a line graph with Taylor Swift’s album ratings in red and Bon Iver’s albums ratings in blue.

![taskartistcomparison5.png](/images/taskartistcomparison5.png)

### Vinyl  
A box containing **Only 10 ratings** is shown. 

1. Click on the box to open a dropdown menu.  
2. Select a minimum rating.  
3. Press **Submit** to show top-rated albums filtered by unowned vinyls.

![taskvinyl1.png](/images/taskvinyl1.png)
Example:   
Find the top rated unowned vinyl albums with a minimum rating of 9\.

1. Click on the box containing **Only 10 ratings**.

![taskvinyl2.png](/images/taskvinyl2.png)

2. Select **9 and higher**.

![taskvinyl3.png](/images/taskvinyl3.png)

3. Click **Submit**. The top ranking unowned vinyl albums with a minimum rating of 9 is displayed

![taskvinyl4.png](/images/taskvinyl4.png)
