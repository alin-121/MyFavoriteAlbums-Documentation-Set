# User Overview
This section describes what you should know when using the web application.

## What is MyFavoriteAlbums?  
MyFavoriteAlbums is a browser based application that analyzes and visualizes album ranking and ranking data. On the site, you can click on the blue tabs at the top of the page to access the following features:  
 ![tasktabs.png](/images/tasktabs.png)

* Number One Albums: Shows the highest rated albums based on an interval of years  
* Top Albums by Year: Shows all ranked albums based on a given year  
* Artists’ Albums: Shows all ranked albums of a chosen artist  
* Favorite Artists: Shows the high rated artists based off of selected minimum albums  
* Artist Comparison: Shows a line graph displaying two artists’ album ratings over time  
* Vinyl: Shows unowned vinyls based off of ratings

---

## Album Information  
MyFavoriteAlbums uses another individual’s ratings and rankings of their favorite albums over the years.

---

## Album Data Usage  
To understand the information presented, certain key definitions will be given to aid the understanding of each feature on the site.

* Album: Title of the album  
* Year: An album’s year of release  
* Ranking: An album’s position relative to other albums in a year based off of rating  
* Artist: The name of the artist/band who released an album  
* Rating: Number personally given to an album on a 0-10 scale  
* Vinyl: Vinyl is owned/unowned by the user  
* Extended Play (EP): A collection of songs but not as many as an album  
* Live: Album recording is live
