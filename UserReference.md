# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
### How do I use my own albums?  
You will need to create your own .csv file and implement your own data. Replace your own .csv file in the **data** folder of MyFavoriteAlbums and change all instances of the “album-rankings.csv” file name to your .csv file name.

> For step-by-step instructions, go to the *Developer Task Documentation* and under *Implementing your own dataset*.

### The website went gray and says: “Disconnected from server.” What should I do?  
Click on **Reload** or reload the page through other means to reconnect to the server. 

### Is there a faster way than scrolling through the drop down menu?  
After clicking on the drop down menu, first press backspace to clear the space and then type in the option you would like to see.
